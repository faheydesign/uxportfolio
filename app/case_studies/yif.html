Year in Focus

Telling a story with pictures

Getty Images wanted a new way to share the best of its content. Traditionally working with mainly a business audience they wanted to engage a consumer audience while not alienating it’s b2b customers.

Leading with Emotion

We decided to organize the site by categories of emotion. We felt this could resonate with consumer users more than the traditional categories of Editorial and Creative.

Capturing an Audience

We knew if we wanted to get consumers’ attention we needed to focus on mobile and tell inspiring stories. We partnered with the editorial team out of New York to create a flexible system that would work for them.

Final Design

After incorporating feedback from development, product and the content team, we deliver a solution that averaged 50,000 page views a day. Marketing through social media helped drive 60% more traffic compared to previous projects.
