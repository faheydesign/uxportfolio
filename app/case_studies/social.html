Social Registration

IMPROVING EXPERIENCE WITH SOCIAL

Getty Images was looking to improve registration to its site by simplifying registration and exploring the power of social to improve its user experience.

EXPLORING THE POSSIBILITIES

We mapped out possible flows with different phases of the project. This enabled UX, product, and development to dig deeper into the project and set timelines. We explored the ways users' interests could tailor their experience. Following photographers, topics and even searches were explored.

Streamlining expectations

The project was scaled back just to cover social registration. We explored using modals to minimize user disruption.

Final Design

After launch, we saw a 30% increase in registration overall with Facebook logins.
