Rio Olympics

Going for the gold

The Olympics were only a few weeks away and Getty Images wanted a way to showcase the best moments of the games to their users.

Built for Exploration

We architected the site so users could explore the content in a variety of filters: by personality, individual sports and countries. The home page is used to feature the variety of the games and other content of the site.

Design Explorations

In designing the site, we tried to capture the energy of the moment of the games. We explored having in depth personality pages of the athletes. We also considered surfacing social media comments and medal counts. Ultimately, they were dropped due to the tight timeline.

Final Design

After incorporating delivered user testing with feedback from development, product and the content team, we deliver a solution that saw a 20% higher rate of interaction. 5% of site traffic saw Olympic content. Landing pages also saw a lower bounce rate than comparable pages.
