Rio Olympics 2016

World class content

Getty Images was looking for a way to feature it's abundant images from the Olympics and draw a new consumer audience.

Attracting a new audience

Traditionaly a company with a B2B model. Getty Images was looking to monetize it's web traffic that was browsing the site by adding advertising.

Wireframes and Flows

Built for Exploration

We architected the site so users could explore the content in a variety of filters: by personality, individual sports and countries. The home page is used to feature the variety of the games and other content of the site.

Design Explorations

In designing the site, we tried to capture the energy of the moment of the games. We explored having in depth personality pages of the atheletes. We also considered surfacing social media comments and medal counts but ultimately they were dropped due to time constraints.
